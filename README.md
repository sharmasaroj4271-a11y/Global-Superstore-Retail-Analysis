# 📊 Global Superstore Retail Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data from a Global Superstore to uncover key business insights related to sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and regional trends.

Using Microsoft Excel, an interactive dashboard was developed to help management monitor KPIs, identify loss-making products, evaluate discount strategies, and make data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The primary objective of this project was to:

* Analyze overall sales and profitability performance.
* Identify high-performing and underperforming products.
* Understand customer purchasing behavior.
* Evaluate the impact of discounts on profitability.
* Compare regional performance.
* Provide actionable recommendations to improve business outcomes.

## 🔍 Key Insights

### Product Analysis

* Technology category generated the highest sales.
* Copiers emerged as the most profitable sub-category.
* Tables generated significant losses despite strong sales volume.

### Discount Analysis

* Orders with discounts above 30% showed a substantial decline in profitability.
* Excessive discounting was identified as a major contributor to losses.

### Regional Analysis

* The Central region recorded the highest losses for Tables.
* Regional performance varied significantly across product categories.

### Customer Analysis

* A small group of customers contributed a large percentage of total revenue.
* Customer concentration highlights opportunities for retention strategies.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### 1. Optimize Discount Strategy

Reduce excessive discounting and establish category-specific discount limits.

### 2. Focus on High-Profit Products

Increase marketing and inventory investment in profitable products such as Copiers and Technology products.

### 3. Improve Loss-Making Categories

Review pricing, supplier contracts, and logistics costs for Tables and other underperforming products.

### 4. Regional Performance Monitoring

Implement monthly profitability reviews at the regional level.

### 5. Customer Retention Programs

Develop loyalty initiatives targeting high-value customers.
